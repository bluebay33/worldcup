World Cup Tracker — Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 23, 2026

We do not collect, store, or share any personal information. No accounts, no login, no ads, and no third-party analytics or tracking.

1. Information we collect

None. The app has no sign-up, no login, and no user profiles. It uses no advertising and no third-party analytics SDKs.

2. Data stored on your device

The app saves a single setting — your language preference — in your device's local storage. It stays on your device and is never sent to us.

3. Region detection

To show a match-highlight link that is playable in your region, the app reads your approximate country code from Cloudflare's edge (/cdn-cgi/trace) within your browser. It is used only to pick a link (e.g., a regional sports channel or a search) and is not stored or transmitted by us.

4. Hosting

The app is hosted on Cloudflare Pages. As with any website, the hosting provider may process standard technical request data (such as IP address) to deliver and secure the service.

5. External links

Highlights open third-party sites (such as YouTube, official broadcasters' channels, or a search engine). We do not control these sites; their own privacy policies apply.

6. Children

This is a general-audience sports information tool and does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children.

7. Changes

We may update this policy; the "last updated" date above will change accordingly.

8. Contact

Questions about this policy: bluebay3333@gmail.com

This app is unofficial and not affiliated with FIFA or any official tournament organizer.

中文

世界杯战报 — 隐私政策

最后更新:2026 年 6 月 23 日

我们不收集、不存储、不分享任何个人信息。无账号、无登录、无广告,也没有任何第三方统计或追踪。

1. 我们收集的信息

没有。本应用无需注册、无需登录、没有用户档案,不使用任何广告或第三方统计 SDK。

2. 存在你设备上的数据

应用仅在你设备的本地存储里保存一项设置——你的语言偏好。它只留在你设备上,绝不会发送给我们。

3. 地区识别

为了给你一个在你所在地区能播放的集锦链接,应用会在你的浏览器内读取 Cloudflare 边缘节点(/cdn-cgi/trace)返回的大致国家代码。它仅用于选择链接(例如某地区的体育频道或搜索),我们不会存储或传输该信息。

4. 托管

本应用托管于 Cloudflare Pages。与任何网站一样,托管商可能为提供和保障服务而处理标准的技术请求数据(如 IP 地址)。

5. 外部链接

集锦会打开第三方网站(如 YouTube、官方转播商频道,或搜索引擎)。我们无法控制这些网站,适用它们各自的隐私政策。

6. 儿童

本应用是面向大众的体育资讯工具,不会有意收集任何人(包括儿童)的数据。

7. 变更

我们可能更新本政策,上方"最后更新"日期会相应变化。

8. 联系方式

关于本政策的疑问:bluebay3333@gmail.com

本应用为非官方应用,与 FIFA 及任何赛事官方机构无关联。
